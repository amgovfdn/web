American Governance Foundation

a non-profit corporation

to study and promote the betterment of public institutions

AGF is a registered 501(c)(3) tax-exempt public charity incorporated in Delaware and headquartered in San Francisco.

AGF organizes and incubates research, policy, and public engagement projects.

Our current focus areas are American grand strategy and great power relations, strategic understanding of China, economic development, political theory, and aesthetics.

Please direct all grant inquiries to grants@americangovernance.org.

AGF Programs

The Center for Strategic Translation is a non-profit research institute that locates, translates, and analyzes strategic Chinese texts to inform scholarship and policymaking.

Our quarterly print publication is Palladium. Palladium publishes weekly long-form journalism and analysis for free online.

We accept tax-deductible donations to advance our mission.
