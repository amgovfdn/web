American Governance Foundation

a non-profit corporation

to study and promote the betterment of public institutions
AGF organizes and incubates research, policy, and outreach projects. We also make grants and sponsor research that advances our mission.

Focus areas: grand strategy and great power relations; developmentalism; epistemic institutions; governance

Better Development studies economic development success stories to learn how developmental governments function and to apply these lessons.

Our quarterly print publication is PALLADIUM.
